# iGosa — Promotional Video Production Brief
## Target: South African Real Estate Agents

---

## VIDEO CONCEPT

**Title:** "Your Phone. Your Business. 24/7."
**Duration:** 90 seconds
**Tone:** Warm, professional, aspirational — not corporate
**Audience:** Independent estate agents in Johannesburg townships and suburbs
**Goal:** "I need this for my business" reaction

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE PROFILE

- Independent estate agent in Soweto/Alexandra/Tembisa/Lenasia
- 25–45 years old
- Uses WhatsApp for 90% of business communication
- Has no website, no CRM
- Gets 20–80 WhatsApp inquiries per week
- Loses leads because they can't respond fast enough
- Operates on commission: R10K–R75K per deal
- Tech-savvy on phone, NOT on computer

---

## KEY MESSAGES (Must Include)

1. **"Your phone now works 24/7"** — Never miss a lead again
2. **"Reply in seconds, not hours"** — Speed wins deals
3. **"It speaks the language of your clients"** — Zulu, English, Sotho, all natural
4. **"Knows Soweto like you do"** — Understands RDP, FLISP, township property
5. **"From R499/month"** — Less than one commission pays for a year
6. **"You focus on selling. We handle the rest."** — Follow-ups, documents, reminders

---

## SCENE-BY-SCENE SCRIPT

### Scene 1: The Problem (0:00–0:15)
**Visual:** Fast cuts of a busy agent. Phone buzzing non-stop. WhatsApp notifications piling up — 47 unread. Agent is at a viewing, can't reply. Text overlays: "47 unread messages." "3 buyers waiting." "1 deal slipping away."

**Voiceover (Zulu-accented, warm):**
"In Jozi, property moves fast. But you're one person. And your phone never stops."

**Sound:** Phone notification sounds, building urgency

---

### Scene 2: The Solution (0:15–0:25)
**Visual:** Same agent's phone. But now — each message gets an instant reply. Split screen: agent doing a viewing on the left, phone auto-responding on the right. WhatsApp bubbles appearing with property details, booking viewings.

**Voiceover:**
"Meet iGosa. Your AI property assistant that lives in your WhatsApp."

**Sound:** Calm transition. "WhatsApp message sent" sound.

---

### Scene 3: How It Works — Buyer Journey (0:25–0:50)
**Visual:** Screen recording of a WhatsApp conversation. Typing animation.

**Buyer message:** "Sawubona, ngifuna i-house eSoweto, 3 bedroom, under R500K"

**iGosa responds (2 seconds):** "Yebo! 🏠 I found 3 options in Soweto under R500K: 1. Pimville — 3 bed, R450K 2. Diepkloof — 3 bed, R480K 3. Meadowlands — 3 bed, R420K Reply with the number to book a viewing! 📅"

**Buyer:** "I like number 1. Can I view it Saturday?"

**iGosa:** "📅 Viewing booked! Saturday 10am at 23 Jabu St, Pimville. Your agent Thabiso will meet you there."

**Voiceover:**
"A buyer messages. iGosa responds in seconds. Searches your listings. Books the viewing. Qualifies the lead. All while you're at another viewing."

**Sound:** Typing sounds, message send pings

---

### Scene 4: The SA Difference (0:50–1:05)
**Visual:** Map of Johannesburg zooming into Soweto. Icons appearing: RDP house, FLISP logo, bond calculator, WhatsApp icon with SA flag colors.

**Voiceover:**
"iGosa isn't just any chatbot. It knows RDP rules. FLISP subsidies. Township property values. It speaks Zulu, Sotho, English — the way your clients speak."

**Sound:** Upbeat, proud — "Proudly South African" feel

---

### Scene 5: Behind the Scenes (1:05–1:20)
**Visual:** Dashboard view. Pipeline cards moving from "Lead" → "Viewing" → "Offer" → "Closed". Stats animating: "12 leads this week." "3 viewings booked." "R29,400 commission."

**Voiceover:**
"While iGosa handles the chat, you see everything in your dashboard. Every lead. Every deal. Every rand of commission. Your business, under control."

**Sound:** Satisfying UI interaction sounds

---

### Scene 6: Call to Action (1:20–1:30)
**Visual:** Agent smiling, phone in hand. Text overlay: "Your Phone. Your Business. 24/7." Then: "iGosa — from R499/month." WhatsApp logo + "Start your free trial: wa.me/27612980377"

**Voiceover:**
"iGosa. Your phone now works 24/7. From R499 a month. Less than half a commission. Start your free trial today."

**Sound:** Warm, confident close. Short music sting.

## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

| Element | Direction |
|---------|-----------|
| Color palette | WhatsApp green (#128C7E), warm amber, South African earth tones |
| Typography | Clean sans-serif (Inter), bold for headlines |
| Motion style | Clean, modern UI animations. Think Apple product videos meets WhatsApp |
| Stock footage | Johannesburg streets, Soweto homes, township scenes, agents with clients |
| Screen recordings | Real WhatsApp conversations in light + dark mode |
| Transitions | Smooth slides, WhatsApp-style message bubbles appearing |
| Text overlays | SA flag color accents, WhatsApp green highlights |

---

## AUDIO STYLE GUIDE

| Element | Direction |
|---------|-----------|
| Voiceover | Female or male SA English with natural accent. Warm, not salesy |
| Background music | Amapiano-influenced but subtle. Upbeat but not distracting |
| Sound effects | WhatsApp message sounds, subtle UI clicks, phone buzzes |
| Pacing | Faster cuts in problem section, smoother in solution section |
